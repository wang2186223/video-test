Kenya Anderson is a 23 year old ballerina, who teaches young girls ballet at a small dance school. She always wanted to get a big break as a lead ballerina but never did, because of the slight limp in her right leg. She usually gets to perform as a back up dancer in the ballet shows. But on this particular show, she attracts the attention of U.S Senatorial aspirant, Levi Ruthford. Teetering both their worlds, completely... Unraveling this mysterious, Senatorial aspirant, Levi Ruthford, bit by tiny bit...
