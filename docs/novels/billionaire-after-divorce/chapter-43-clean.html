Chapter43 The box Mother left behind

"Ever since Brady threw me to Novaria three years ago. I've been preparing for any possible mishaps. Do you remember the largest partner company we had overseas?" Ethan nodded, asking in return.

“Do you mean that offshore investment company?" Jayden seemed to have guessed something and was surprised.

Ethan explained while smiling, "Yes, that's my Plan B! Almost all of Cloud Group's investments in the past three years were established through that company. As a third-party and the largest investor in all those projects,they can withdraw funds at any time and leave the mess to Cloud Group!"

"Back then, I never understood why you insisted on collaborating with such a shady foreign company.I didn't expect it to be yours!" Jayden had now understood Ethan's plan.

Once this company withdrew all of its investments, Cloud Group would face defaults and enormous losses.It wouldn't be impossible for it to collapse within a short time. Moreover, the pile-up of bad debts and legal suits from partnerships will make Cloud Group panic.

Jayden knew Ethan had a backup plan, but he didn't expect it to be so big and decisive!

Ethan put down his chopsticks and approached the window, looking at the moon in the sky. His gaze became sharper.

"Like I said, it'd be impossible for Brady to get something with nothing. Since he wants Cloud Group,I'll give it to him! However, what he'll get is a ticking time bomb!

Ethan's words shocked Jayden even more.

His plan wasn't just aimed at Cloud Group, but at the Wilson family's entire business empire!

Once Cloud Group collapsed or even went bankrupt, it would be a huge blow to the entire family,even affecting their work with other businesses. Their total assets would fall by at least 5 to 10percent!

Although it sounded small, less than a tenth, it would still be a major blow to a large corporation like the Wilsons. Their business empire would be greatly affected.

In terms of big issues, this was uandoubtedly a terrifying number. Once this occurs, it would definitely make Brady lose his temper and greatly affect hisreputation in the Wilson family!

"Sir, if you do that, the consequences...“

Jayden's face contorted. The consequences he mentioned weren't about the Wilson family's losses,but their subsequent anger!

"That's also my biggest concern, so we must do this at the right time! It's either we strike while the iron's hot,or we just don't!"

Ethan snorted coldly, clenching his fists tightly.

"However, we wouldn't be able to withstand their anger right now, especially since Mike is a major threat now!"Jayden couldn't help but remind.

Upon hearing this, Ethan sighed helplessly. "That's why I want power, enough to confront the Wilson family's stength! However, I can't rush in doing this."

If Mike reappeared,with Ethan's current strength, he wouldn't stand a chanceand could only be killed.

This powerlessness made him feel frustrated and deeply helpless.

After brief silence, Jayden suddenly said, "Sir, I think it's time I give you this thing I've been keeping."

Ethan was surprised as he asked,"What is it?"

“...It's a box that Madam entrusted me to keep. I'm sorry. Sir, for hiding this from you all this time. I did it under Madam's request. One time, she told me to only hand this over to you when there's no other choices in a crisis.“

Jayden explained through clenched teeth.

"My mother? Why are you telling me this now?" Ethan's face turned pale as he asked.

He had always trusted Jayden, and the latter knew how much he wanted to find any clues about his mother.

Despite that, he hid this information about his mother for so many years!

"I'm sorry. Sir! I merely followed Madam's orders, and I never wanted to keep this from you! I won't complain if you're angry or want to scold me!"

Jayden immediately kneeled on the ground, resolutely apologizing.

This instantly diminished Ethan's anger by half. He also knew that since this his mother specially requested this, Jayden wasn't the one at fault.

"Alright, get up and tell me what exactly happened. When did my mother give you this box, and what did she say?"

Ethan waved his hand, urgently asking.

After so many years, he finally received some news about her. However, he never thought she'd left him something!

"It was just a few days before the car accident. At that time, Madam suddenly called for me and gave me this small box:"

Jayden slowly began to explain.

"I didn't think much of it at the time since she was still..."

"Anyway, I agreed to do it. I never expected that she'd be in a car accident later,and only then did I realize that she probably sensed that she was in danger. Therefore, she carved a way out for you! However.I can't understand why Madam, who has always been with the Wilsons and didn't contact the outside world much.would do such a thing."

"It must be related to the Wilsons. I must find out the truth behind this accident. To do that, I need enough power to defeat the Wilsons and force Brady to reveal the truth!"

Ethan clenched his fist tightly. Only with this could he find clues about his mother and figure everything out!

“Oh. where's the box you mentioned?"

At this moment, he suddenly remembered the box and quickly asked Jayden.

"Please wait a moment, I'll go get it for you!" Jayden said and got up to leave the room.

Shortly after, he returned while holding a dark box that had a palm-sized eagle pattern.

"Is this the box?" Ethan accepted the box and carefully examined it.

However, after looking at it for a while, he noticed that it couldn't be opened at all. One side of it had a keyhole, clearly indicating that it needed a key to open.

"What's inside? Where's the key? "Ethan asked.

"I'm sorry, Sir. I aIso don't know what's inside since I've never opened it before. When Madam gave me this,she never gave me the key. She said that she already gave it to you!" Jayden shook his head and explained while staring at Ethan.

“She already gave it to me?” Ethan was taken aback by the news.

A memory of a conversation with his mother suddenly flashed in his mind. Ethan's gaze fell on his chest.on an amber stone pendant his mother gave him years ago, instructing him to keep it safe. Turns out it was a key to unlocking a treasure!

Afterward, he thought that his mother was probably joking around.

After realizing this, Ethan immediately took out the amber stone from his chest and fiercely smashed it on the ground, cracking it open and revealing a thin and long metal object inside.
