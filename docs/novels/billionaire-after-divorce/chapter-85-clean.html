Chapter 82

Seeing that Ethan didn't want to say much, despite her curiosity,Hannah could only leave.

Back at her place, she laid down on bed after showering. She was still contemplating Ethan's unbelievable identity!

On the other hand, Ethan was in his living room as he observed everything outside through a gap in the curtains, his eyes flashing with a hint of sharpness!

When he got out of the car earlier, he felt someone was watching him from behind. Now, he saw several figures hidden in the darkness, revealing their identities.

Just then, his phone suddenly rang, indicating a text message.

He took out his phone and saw a message from an unknown number.Its contents were brief, [Your location has been revealed. Leave immediately!]

Ethan's expression immediately turned strange. He realized that he had been exposed, but who was this person warning him? Could someone within the Wilsons be secretly helping him?

At that moment, the figures in the darkness began to move, swiftly scaling the wall into the villa yard.

Based on their performance, they were undoubtedly skilled.

They are all of the Wilson family's elite force!

Ethan smirked slightly as he put away his phone.

Why should he withdraw? Why should he leave?

If it were before, he would've avoided anyone related to the Wilson family,but now... He had the confidence to face Mike head-on! This confidence naturally came from the bead and the mysterious aura that had just strengthened up today!

These strong assassins used to give him a headache, but now, it was uncertain who would win or lose!

Under the dark night, the eight assassins sneaked into the villa's backyard and checked the situation inside before quietly prying opening the front door.

At this moment, Mike and two of his men appeared at the entrance,pushing Tim along.

"Is he still inside?" Mike squinted and asked his subordinate.

His subordinate quickly reported, “After returning home, he's been staying inside the whole time without leaving. Moreover, that woman,Hannah,also came once and left shortly after!"

“You,take care of the young master, the rest of us will go in together!"Mike casually ordered before entering the villa.

He didn't think it was necessary to be cautious when dealing with Ethan.

Perhaps those eight infiltrators had already immobilized Ethan on the ground!

However, just as Mike's group reached the center of the courtyard, all the lights suddenly dimmed and plunged the entire place into darkness,as if there was a power outage!

Hannah's villa still had lights on next door, so it clearly wasn't the case.

"What's going on?" The sudden darkness surprised Tim, nervously asking in a trembling voice.

Mike couldn't help but show a look of disgust. This waste was actually the Wilson family's young master, truly a laughingstock!

“You watch the young master. Wait for me here!" He didn't bother with this waste and continued walking.

Without any light, only the faint moonlight filtered through the villa's front door into the living room.

Although it was dim, Mike's sharp gaze still cut through the darkness and instantly spotted four figures lying on the ground!

From the living room to the staircase, all four people were lying in the same direction, causing Mike's expression to change.

He didn't hear these four being handle. There were no movements from outside, and none of the thugs made any noise. Mike was surprised!

He was well aware of Ethan's strength, and it was impossible for him to silently take care of these eight people! Could there be someone else secretly helping him?

A faint sound came from upstairs.

Mike narrowed his eyes and walked up the stairs in that direction.

Along the way, he saw four other people, from the stairs all the way to the second-floor corridor.

All of them were motionless on the ground!

“Ethan, I really underestimated you. Since you've got back-up, then why are you being so secretive?" After confirming that all eight of his subordinates had failed, Mlike's face turned ugly as he coldly spoke in the dark, as if Ethan stood right in front of him.

As soon as said that, the villa's power was restored, and all the lights came back on.

With a stern expression, Mike turned around and saw Ethan standing at the other end of the corridor, looking at him with a faint smile.

He suspiciously asked, "Where are your helpers?" Ethan sneered contemptuously,"Would I need help dealing with them?Your followers are useless, getting knocked out by just one punch each.I didn't even put much effort into it!"

Once these eight men entered the villa, he had deliberately cut off the electric and turned the whole place dark.

With his current speed, it was as easy as pie to approach and strike these lackeys,especially in the dark. They neither had time to react nor could they see their companions, so Ethan annihilated them one by one within a matter of seconds.

For Ethan, they were just experiments, and the results were more than satisfactory.

“Are you saying you did all this?"

Mike's pupils widened, his rationality telling him it was impossible for Ethan to accomplish this!

Ethan sarcastically mocked,"Are you shocked by this? You were even worried that I had someone in the dark to ambush you. I'm really honored that someone the Wilsons have trained thinks so highly of me!"

“Do you think that by defeating these wastes, you can run from me? Last time you were lucky, but you won't be so fortunate this time!" Mike stared at Ethan maliciously, as if he wanted to decipher Ethan's expression.

However, after observing for a while, Mike was disappointed. He couldn't determine if Ethan was bluffing or not!

"Since you're so confident, then I won't stop you. I'll be waiting for you outside. If you're able to, come out and fight me!" Ethan's confidence and disdain completely bewildered Mike. Where did his arrogance come from?

However, he didn't have much time to ponder as Ethan directly went downstairs!

Was thisman really alone? How did he manage to do all of this? Even if his strength had improved in these few days, it couldn't have improved this rapidly, no?

He only silently took care of these eight lackeys, nothing more!

Suddenly, Mike's expression changed. He recalled that useless Tim was still at the villa's entrance!
