Chapter 83

As the villa's lights returned, Tim in the courtyard urgently ordered his men to push him inside.

However, Ethan suddenly appeared at that moment.

He paused for a moment when he saw Tim, who looked like a mummy sitting in a wheelchair. If he didn't look closely, he wouldn't have believed his eyes.

“Oh, it's just the Wilson family's arrogant and domineering young master!"

“Ethan! How'd you get out? Where's Mlike?”

At this moment, Tim wished he could twist and peel Ethan's tendons.However, he felt anxious as he didn't see Mike anywhere!

"I thought it was someone else. Anyway, since you've had this attitude from the start, I hope you never change!”

Ethan chuckled. He didn't expect his kick from last time to be so strong.Tim must've broken several bones when he landed!

“F*ck you, Ethan! Today, I won't let you live, nor will I let you die!" Tim angrily cursed, but he only got Ethan's dumbfounded look.

Just then, Mike suddenly jumped from the balcony and landed,standing between the two.

"Why would you jump off when there's a proper way down? Are you that worried I'll kill this idiot?" Ethan squinted his eyes as he saw Mike,asking in a casual tone.

Mike coldly said, "Just you? Stop dreaming!”

"Is that so? Then let mne see if you can match up to your words!" Ethan snorted, rushing fiercely toward Tim as soon as he finished speaking. His speed was so fast that a normal person wouldn't be able to react in time! However, Mike was standing between him and Tim.

“You're asking for it!"

Mike's face turned cold. Amazed by Ethan's speed, he was also infuriated.He threw a punch toward the other party.

The moment their fists clashed, tremendous force erupted from the recoil.

Accompanied by crackling sounds from their joints, Mike took three steps back, while Ethan retreated more than a dozen steps before finally standing pale-faced and steady.

"You were hiding your strength all along?" Although Mike remained composed, his eyes flickered with uncertainty!

The strength Ethan displayed far exceeded his expectations. If he left this man alone, Ethan might become unbeatable one day!

“Believe what you want. Why should I answer you?" Ethan's face turned ugly as he coldly snorted, feeling the energy in his body churn as it suppressed the impact Mike caused.

This clash made him realize the gap between his and the enemy's strength without any energy!

The result wasn't hard to accept. At least, he had some self-defense power in front of Mike!

It also made him realize that defeating Mike couldn't be achieved head-on.He needed to make Mike lower his defenses and catch him off-guard!

"Hahaha! You really overestimate yourself, Ethan! Do you think you can fight against the Shadow Gang just because you learned some fighting skills? Let me tell you, you can't do anything except surrender in front of Mike. Now be smart and kneel down to apologize! Maybe I'll make your death quicker!" Seeing this situation, Tim laughed triumphantly,feeling a sense of revenge.

"What if I don't?”Ethan squinted his eyes, sneering coldly. “Still resisting, huh? Then I'll let Mike take care of you first,break each of your bones one-by-one, and make you beg for life and long for death!After I'm done toying with you, I'll personally slaughter you!" Tim's eyes reddened, gritting his teeth. The bandages on his face concealed his hideous expression.

“Then lethim try. If he fails, I'll be the first one to deal with you,just like you said earlier!" Ethan replied dismissively with a light grunt.

At this moment, Hannah heard the commotion from the neighboring villa.She ran over, apparently alarmed by the noises!

"Stop! Who are you people? I've already called the police, and they'll arrive soon if you don't leave!" her sudden appearance surprised Mike and the others, and even Ethan was also taken aback. This foolish woman was risking her life by rushing out like this!

Seeing the situation, Tim sneered unpleasantly,“Calling the police? What a joke. Try asking the cops if they'd dare intervene in the Wilson family's affairs! Ethan, I never thought you would stoop so low as to rely on a foolish woman to stand up for you. However, I must say that she is quite stunning. I envy you!"

Ethan warned Hannah,“Why are you here? Go back quickly! Calling the police won't work against these people. They wouldn't even dare to come!"

Hannah insisted stubbornly, “No, I can't just stand by and watch you in trouble!”

“What a joke. Once you're here, you won't be able to leave! Ethan, since you don't want to kneel down for me, should I just grab this woman and let my boys play with her? When they're done, I'll sell her into slavery.What do you say?" Tim smirked darkly, as if he had control over everything. On the other hand, Mike was disgusted by Tim's method of using a woman to threaten Ethan, but he could only frown in silence. He didn't want to stoop to such low measures! “You can try. I'll send you straight into a coffin!" Ethan retorted with a cold look, while Hannah's face turned pale.

“I'm indeed surprised by your strength, but you'restill no match for me!Ethan, persisting against me isn't a wise choice; you'll only bring harm to others!" Mike warned with narrowed eyes. His target was solely Ethan;he had no intention of harming others,especially a woman!

“It's too late to regret now. Mike, you said the Miller family's people were off-limits, so do you have no objections if I mess with this one? You twO,go and get that woman for me!" Without any hesitation,Tim arrogantly commanded his two henchmen behind him.

Mike's face immediately darkened, but even though he wasn't pleased with Tim's order, he could only remain silent.

Upon hearing Mike's silence, the two men quickly moved toward Hannah.

"You're really asking for it!" Ethan's face suddenly changed, immediately shielding Hannah.

Perhaps he wasn't Mike's match, but he was more than enough to deal with these two men.

Before the two guys reached Hannah, Ethan grabbed one of the two's arms and pulled him back, while he kicked the other one away!

Then, Ethan followed-up with a punch to the other man's right in front of him. He easily defeated both men, confirming to Mike that everything in the villa was indeed his own doing, without anyone else helping him.

“You're the one asking for it here!" Mike immediately shouted and rushed toward Ethan, his speed leaving no room for Ethan to react.

With Hannah's scream, Ethan could only instinctively sense the danger,his face contorting.
