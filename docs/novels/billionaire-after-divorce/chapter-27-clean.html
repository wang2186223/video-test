Chapter27 Apologies

Kevin looked around, with everyone instinctively lowering their heads. Even the restaurant staff didn't dare step forward and become involved in this unfortunate situation.

At this moment, Evan secretly smiled and stepped forward to explain, “Boss, I'm Evan fromn Cloud Group's subsidiary company!We met at the company's annual meeting before... You still remember,right?”

He continued,"It all started when my woman came here and this guy bumped into her. Not only did he not apologize, but he also insulted her. Therefore, I came here to make him apologize, but he started causing a scene. Manager Caleb also tried to intervene, but ended up getting severely injured...”

Following Evan's direction, Kevin looked over and saw Caleb lying on the ground in a miserable state. His face changed colors as he said,"Splendid, just amazing. It's been years since someone last dared to cause trouble in my place!"

Regardless of who was right or wrong, or the reasons behind it. how dare someone lay hands on the customers in his restaurant? They simply didn't want to live!

"Yes, Boss! They even slapped mel These kinds of arrogant scum caused trouble in your restaurant, so they must be severely punished!" Anastasia also walked over, showing her red face.

"He's lying! That's not what happened at all, because they're the ones who started it!”

At this moment, Chloe quickly exclaimed, “Evan is making stuff up!It was that woman who caused trouble! And earlier, he and the manager said such offensive and insulting words to us, but now they're blaming us for everything!"

"Don't worry, I'll give everyone an explanation regarding this matter,"

Kevin looked at her, his face dark and his eyes icy, “Did you all really start a fight just now?"

If this incident didn't get a proper resolution, how could he continue to establish himself with the locals?

Chloe bit her lip, not knowing how to explain.

"No need to waste your breath with them. I made the first move.”

"What about your hand?” Before Chloe could finish speaking,Ethan, who had been facing away from Kevin all this time, finally turned around and took a step forward.

Upon hearing these arrogant words, the guests present Wasn't he afraid of divine punishment? Does he not fear death?

For a moment, the entire room fell silent, and everyone was eager to see what cruel means Kevin would use to take care of Ethan.

Kevin was burning with anger. Just as he was about to order someone to punish Ethan, he swallowed his words back when he saw Ethan's face clearly.

Wasn't this Ethan Wilson? Cloud Group's mysterious chairman he had heard of?!

He was the boss of his boss, and it wouldn't be an overstatement to say that he was his lifeline!

This mysterious chairman wasn't typically known even to the company executives. Kevin himself had only met Ethan by chance once before, never expecting to meet him again in his own restaurant and in such a situation!

If Ethan were to get angry, not only would Kevin lose his position,but his future would also be in jeopardy!

Cold sweat instantly broke out on his forehead as he hurriedly walked over.

Seeing him walking straight toward Ethan, many people began to sneer.

This guy was indeed bold, actually daring to start a fight in Sunrise Restaurant.

Moreover, the person who had been fighting seemed to have an extraordinary background. Based on his speech, he seemed to know Kevin!

This matter was about to escalate completely!

Evan and Anastasia looked at Ethan with scorn in their eyes. Ethan had been arrogant, and now he would get what he deserved. Now, it was time for him and Chloe to suffer!

Even Caleb,who was lying on the ground with a broken skull and bleeding,stared at Ethan with eyes full of resentment. He wanted to see Kevin kill Ethan!

Contrary to their expectations, Kevin quickly wwalked up to Ethan,slightly bowing as he respectfully said,"Sir, what exactly happened here? Did those people disturb your meal?”

Seeing this, everyone was stunned. A strange silence filled the room.

No one had expected the situation to develop like this.

Not only did Kevin not punish Ethan severely, but he also respectfully asked him what happened?

What was going on?

Chloe,sitting across from Ethan, just as confused.

She thought that Ethan was done for!

Kevin was smart. He knew that Ethan kept his identity a secret from the world, so he kept his mouth shut.

"Not only did they interrupt my meal, but Caleb, as the manager,dared to bully the weak in public, trying to make my friend sleep with that scumbag. Tell me, does your restaurant not have any rules?" Ethan's tone was calm, but it sounded like a heavy bombshell in Kevin's ears!

How dare these bastards try to steal Mr. Wilson's woman?

They didn't even have enough to lives to make up for their deaths!

"Is that so?!" Kevin's gaze turned ice-cold as he looked back at Evan, Anastasia, and Caleb on the ground.

Upon hearing Kevin's words, Evan's face turned pale as he quickly

said,"Boss, don't listen to this kid's nonsense...”

“Shut up!” Kevin turned his head, glaring at Evan with a dark expression. “Get over here and apologize to this gentleman and this lady immediately!"

Kevin's voice was stern.

"Boss...” Evan was baffled, wanting to explain something, However,when he saw Kevin's gloomy face, he immediately shut his mouth.He also knew he had no choice.

If he offended Kevin, the latter could easily destroy him with his position in Cloud Group!

"Evan, why should we apologize to these losers? I don't want to!"Anastasia protested unwillingly.

Although Evan was also from Cloud Group, Kevin actually helped an outsider instead of his own people!

“Shut up and apologize to them!" Evan shouted angrily.

"You don't want to? Fine," Kevin sneered.“I'll report your behavior to those above, which has already seriously affected Cloud Group's image. At that point, not only will you lose your position,but there will also be a sky-high compensation fee. Sunrise Restaurant will do everything in its power to destroy your family!”

Upon hearing Kevin's words, Evan's heart trembled.

With the man's position, he could definitely accomplish what he said!

Although their family is wealthy, their assets were only a few tens of millions of dollars. If Kevin suppressed them, how could they survive?

It would mean death!

Thinking of this, Evan immediately knelt down with a thump

"I'm sorry! I was wrong!"

“Please forgive me, both of you. I'm just a foul-mouthed jerk!”

“I'm a scumbag!"

"I offended you earlier, and I truly apologize!"

As he apologized, he knocked his head on the ground with a loud banging sound.

Chloe was also stunned, as if in a dream.

This man from Cloud Group actually knelt down and apologized to her?
