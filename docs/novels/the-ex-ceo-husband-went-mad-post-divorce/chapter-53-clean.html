Chapter 51

Ella went limp and collapsed onto the bed.

Her hair was all over the place, and even with sunglasses and a mask on.Arthur could picture her flushed little face.

His big hand wandered over her chest and gave it a pinch. "Remember,don't touch any other man from now on!"

"You..."

Ella slapped his hand away. "Stay away from me!"

His phone rang.

Arthur glanced at it and answered, "What's up?"

A sweet voice came through. "Arthur!"

Arthur's expression changed, and he walked to the balcony with the phone.

Ella looked at his indifferent back and sneered, "Heartless man!"

She got dressed, glanced at Arthur's back, and turned to leave.

On the balcony, Arthur was unaware she had left. On the phone, he frowned."You can buy whatever you want."

After saying that, he hung up the phone, turned back to look at the bed,and froze.

The bed was empty, and Ella had snuck awvay.

He curled his lips into a playful smile. "You think you can disobey me.

huh?"

Just as Ella walked out of the room, she got lost again and suddenly sneezed.

She touched her nose, annoyed. "Was this house designed just to make me get lost? Everything looks the same!"

In the vast estate, each corridor was adorned with different paintings, but they all looked the same.

She wandered for a long time but couldn't find the studio.

Just as she was hesitating whether to call for help, she heard a familiar voice.

Around the corner in the lounge, Ava, who had just hung up the phone with Arthur, crossed her legs and raised her chin arrogantly. "You heard what Arthur said just now, right?"

Blair, sitting on the opposite sofa, flattered her. "Of course, Mr. Smith is incredibly generous to you; he even bought a multimillion-dollar necklace for you without a second thought!"

"Of course!" Ava smiled charmingly. "As soon as he knew I came to this show, he invested billions just to make me comfortable in the crew!"

She sighed as she picked up her coffee. "Unfortunately, some woman is shameless and seduces him!"

"Don't worry, leave her to me!" Blair vowed. "I'll make sure she's ruined and never bounces back!"

Ava smiled lightly and handed over a box to Blair, "I really liked you the

moment I saw you; this is for you!"

There were limited edition earrings worth six figures in the box.

Blair accepted them with a smile. "Thanks. As for the film festival..."

Her implication was clear.

Ava nodded confidently. "Leave it to me. I receive several invitations every year anyway!"

"Alright,I'll leave first!"

Blair left with the earrings, smiling.

Ella stood in the corner watching this scene, feeling only irony.

'Who did they call shameless? Could it be her? They are so absurd.

She originally wanted to leave quietly, but suddenly her foot slipped, and she stumbled.

Her body hit the wall, making a sound.

Ava,who was about to leave, heard the noise and walked over to Ella.

As their gazes met, Ava sneered, "What are you doing? Eavesdropping on our conversation!"

"I was listening openly!" Ella stood her ground. "Besides, if you were discussing secrets, you should have found a secluded corner, not the lounge!"

"Where did you go just now?" Ava asked, catching a familiar scent.

She suddenly stepped forward, grabbed Ella's clothes, and tore them,

revealing fresh bite marks on her skin.

Ava's face turned ugly. "What's going on? You're really shameless,having sex with someone during lunch break?"

"Let go of me!" Ella pushed her away and tidied her clothes.

Then she thought of something and deliberately pointed at the exposed bite marks. "Do you know where these marks came from?"

Ava asked nervously, "What do you want to say?"

"They were bitten by a dog!" Ella stepped closer to Ava, forcing her into a corner. "And it was your favorite dog that bit me!"

Could the dog be Arthur? When he was on the phone just now, he seemed impatient, his breathing was a bit rapid, and she even carries his scent. There is something between them.'

Various thoughts intertwined in Ava's mind, making her furious.She raised her hand to hit Ella only to find Ella had already left.

"Bitch!" She stomped her foot in anger, but she did it too hard, and her shoe accidentally hit the wall.

Hearing the curses from around the corner, Ella kept walking and accidentally found her way back to the studio.

"Where did you go? Something seems off?" Lily looked at Ella,who seemed furious, and sniffed her carefully. "Oh, my god, did you two have sex again in such a short time?"

"Stop joking!" Ella lay on the sofa, exhausted. "I was just bitten by a dog!"

"Alright, don't be mad!" Lily playfully pressed Ella's body a couple of

times. "You might consider my suggestion, conquering him through sex is also a skill!"

"Please,give me a break!" Ella said, closing her eyes, planning to take a nap.

In the afternoon, the live broadcast continued.

In front of the camera, Ava was gentle, proper, and playful, interacting with the viewers through comments, while Ella and Lily were busy working.

The initial cutting was completed in the morning, and in the afternoon,they had to make the clothes.

Students who graduated in fashion design would use related tools to make clothes, Ella was no exception.

She wore sunglasses and a mask, although her vision was obstructed,her movements were swift.

In front of the camera, though her face was obscured, her graceful movements garnered acclaim from many viewers.

[You're so beautiful, even though we can't see your face, your movements are elegant!]

[It's been an hour since the broadcast started, and you've been working non-stop,amazing!]

[Ms. Worry-Free, you're great, you don't rely on looks to succeed. I asked around yesterday, and many of my friends like you!]

Seeing the viewers' praise, Ava's face stiffened, and she finally handed a

cup of coffee to Ella. "Ms. Worry-Free, how about taking a break?"

"Thank you, but no need!" It was a critical stage to make the clothes, and Ella couldn't be distracted. She calmly replied and continued working.

Ava still stood there with the coffee. "Have a sip, it's my treat!"

"No, thank you!" Ella politely replied again.

Seeing Ava standing there motionless, Lily came over. "The clothes are almost done, don't cause trouble here,go away!"
