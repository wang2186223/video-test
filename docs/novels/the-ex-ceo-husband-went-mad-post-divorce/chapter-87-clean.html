Chapter 85

The vibe in the live stream room was super tense.

Fans from both sides were at it again, arguing non-stop.

Ella had no clue about any of this, while Arthur kept glancing between the live stream and Ella.

He opened his mouth, like he wanted to say something.

Ava looped her arm through his, stood on her tiptoes, and whispered something in his ear.

The camera caught this moment, and it quickly became a hot topic.

[That's so cute! They look like a couple whispering sweet nothings to each other.]

Lily,who was scrolling through the comments, couldn't take it anymore.

She wanted to rush over and give Ava a piece of her mind.

But she knew she couldn't.

She walked over to Ella and pinched her. "Hey, take a look at this!"

Ella winced in pain and looked up, confused. "What are you talking about?

"Check out the comments!"

"Alright!"

Following Lily's direction, Ella glanced at the comments.

Fans from both sides were bickering, how boring.

She waved at the camera. "Hey everyone, let's keep things cool and not argue. How about I show you a special skill?"

She grabbed a sketchbook and a pen and sat in front of the big screen.

"Let me show you my drawing skills! I have no inspiration right now and need to clear my head!"

Ella's slender fingers swiped across the screen.

Soon,she found a photo of Arthur and Ava whispering among the comments.

Ella was speechless.

They looked so close, like a real couple. No one would believe they were just friends.

She channeled hner frustration into her art, picked up the pen, and started drawing.

[Oh my god, she's amazing! Look at how she sketched the rough outline with just a few strokes! Quick strokes, rich imagination, smooth lines! As an art student myself, I think she's incredible!]

[Look, she actually captured that intimate moment between those two!]The live stream room was buzzing, and the photographer knew exactly who to focus on.

As a result, the number of viewers in the live stream room shot up,quickly hitting hundreds of thousands.

Ella smiled as she drew and explained simply and easily so non-art

students could follow along.

Even though Ella was bundled up, making it hard to see her face,she was shining brightly.

Ava clenched her fists in anger, watching Arthur also staring at Ella,feeling frustrated.

A few minutes later.

Ella finished the drawing and handed it to Ava with a smile. "See if it looks like you!"

The drawing was incredibly lifelike, capturing every gesture and expression,even the hair strands had a sense of spirit.

If this drawing wasn't done by Ella, it would have been even better.

Ava thanked her insincerely. "Thanks!"

"No problem! This is my gift to both of you!"

She emphasized each word slowly.

Arthur frowned, looking annoyed. "We're live streaming now,you should be drawing design sketches, not wasting time on this!"

He snatched the drawing from Ava's hand, folded it, and put it în his pocket.

"Alright, you guys chat, I'll get back to work!"

After the little drama, Ella returned to her desk.

She tried to find some inspiration from Ava and Arthur.

But her eyes were getting tired, and her mind was getting more and more chaotic.

Compared to Ella's struggle, the other two live stream rooms were buzzing with activity.

Cecilia was already married and had kids.

Even though she didn't design a lot of kids' clothes, being a mom herself.Cecilia had whipped up a few outfits for her own kid, so she was pretty good at the parent-child series.

In no time, she had several outfits sketched out, and she even had some ideas for colors and accessories in her head.

Magnolia, who was right next to her, kept giving her thumbs up.

"Your designs are gorgeous! If these hit the market, they'll sell like crazy!"

"You really know how to flatter!"

Cecilia patted Magnolia's shoulder. "How about a challenge? I'll sketch another one, and you handle the color scheme!"

"Really? Thanks for the opportunity!"

"Don't mention it, we're a team!"

With a few quick strokes, Cecilia designed another outfit.

She handed over the color scheme and accessories to Magnolia.

The two of them worked together seamlessly, earning the title of the most harmonious duo from the viewers. In another live stream room. Andy's parent-child outfits were a bit on the immature side.

But the style was bold, and the colors were bright. These clothes might not be a hit domestically, but abroad, they'd definitely be in high demand.

Viewers were hopping between the live stream rooms,checking out all the action.

The afternoon flew by.

The live stream ended.

Ella held her pen, but her paper was still blank.

Lily looked at her sympathetically. "You're just making things hard for yourself! Have a rest!"

"Okay!"

After work, Ella was exhausted. As she passed by the director, she suddenly stopped.

"Hey,director,where's Leo?"

"We had a work adjustment, and he was transferred to Cecilia's team!"

"Why..."

Ella stopped mid-sentence, smiled at the director. "Thanks for letting me know!"

She believed in finishing what she started.

She had promised to design a wedding dress for Leo's fiancée, so she

couldn't go back on her word. She handed her computer and other things to Lily. "You go home first, I have something to do!"

"Alright, keep your phone with you, so you don't get lost!"

"Got it, you go on ahead!"

Ella scanned the crowd.

Leo was tall and handsome, easy to spot in the crowd.

Ella waved in his direction, and Leo quickly ran over when he saw her.

"Why are you running? You're at work, be more steady!" Ella, like a big sister, took out a tissue and handed it to him.

Leo chuckled. "I'm just in a hurry! If you didn't come to find me, I would ve come to find you. My fiancée sent me a message! Let's find a place to talk!"

The estate had many pavilions surrounded by beautiful scenery.

The two of them looked around and walked to the nearest pavilion.

Leo couldn't wait to take out his phone and relay his fiancée's ideas for the wedding dress.

Ella was speechless.

This client had way too many demands.

She wanted the dress to reflect their childhood friendship, romance, beauty,passion, strength, and courage.

'My God! What do young people want these days, to turn a wedding dress

into armor?'

Ella carefully said, "Her demands are a bit too much,can she specify a type, like a magazine she saw?"

Such disorganized ideas made it hard to start.

Leo scratched his head in embarrassment. "I said the same,but she said there's only one wedding, and she must have a perfect dress!"

The more Ella listened to Leo's fiancée's demands for the wedding dress,the more confused she became.

Helpless, she could only keep asking, "Well, did she mention any specific type she likes?"
