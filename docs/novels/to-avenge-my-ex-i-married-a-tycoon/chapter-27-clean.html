Chapter 27

In her apartment, as soon as they got inside,she launched onto the sofa forgetting that Nathan was actually followwing right beside her.

She was tired after what went on during the day. Seems like she is a b*stard with no family. Would Nathan divorce her if he found out about it?

Thinking about this, she totally forgot about the main reason why she was back at her apartment.

Nathan stared at her as he leaned on the door. Taking in all the changes in her expression. What did she go through this past few hours when I left for the hospital?

Looking at the packed luggage's, Nathan cleared his throat and said in a much gentler tone. "Pumpkin, are you taking all this along?"

"Huh?" That was when Hilda recalled her reason for coming back to her apartment. "Yes."

"But they are far too much or what do you think?" The apartment isn't that spacious but she got so many stuffs.Nathan thought to himself.

"How about this, we would still be renting the place so why not just take the most important stuffs you need. The clothes,

you already have a set delivered to my residence so you don't have to take any." Nathan said calmly as he tried his best to sound her out. After all if she says she was going to take all this with her, she wouldn't even use them.

"Erm okay. If you say so."

With that Nathan excited the apartment with a few luggage's and handed them to his bodyguard's before entering back inside as Hilda was unpacking some stuffs and covering all the sofa's.

After an hour, everything was set for them to leave. Walking out of her apartment, she looked back and though. I never thought a day would come like this when I would move out to leave with someone else apart from Daniel. But what can I say.Fate is really cruel.

All the memories that came with the Adams family and this apartment would be locked away somewhere deep within me.As I leave now, I'm going to start a new life as I am now married. There wouldn't be any looking back.

With tears in her eyes, Hilda entered the Maybach before it drove off.

Meanwhile still in the club, Rex was fuming with rage as he was taken away by his mom. "Mom, you have to avenge me.

He broke my leg! He wanted to kill me. I want both of his legs broken too."

Mrs. Cross was infuriated when she received Rex's call telling her someone almost killed him which resulted in her rushing to the club. "Don't worry son. How is it possible that there is someone who dares to make a move on my son? One that could have almost left him crippled? This person deserves nothing but death. Tell me who it is." Mrs. Cross's voice was cold.

Rex was ecstatic when he heard his mom's cold voice. He pretended to speak in a frightened tone. "Mom, he claims he is Mr. Nathan and even asked the guards to ban me from coming to any of their clubs. He was bold enough to threaten me with Mr. Nathan and broke my leg."

Boom.

As soon as Mrs. Cross heard that, she was like a ferocious tiger that has been suppressing her fiery anger.

"Mom you have to avenge me. HHe even said that the Cross family means nothing to him. We should send someone to capture him. I want to give him a beating so that he gets a taste of what it feels like to have one's leg broken." Rex continued

Mrs. Cross was infuriated. Like who was bold enough to say

that the Cross family means nothing to him? Unless that person was courting death. Turning to her bodyguards, he ordered coldly. "You all go bring the..."

Before she could finish, her phone rang. taking it out.she noticed it was from Mr. Cross.

Immediately she answer, the angry roar of Mr. Cross was heard. "You b*tch! I have been telling you to keep your son out of trouble but you wouldn't listen. You see what he has done now?"

Shocked Mrs. Cross looked at the Id and made sure it was her husband. "Hun, what are you talking about? What has Rex done? He is here with me at the moment in the hospital so what are you talking about?"

"What am I talking about? Why not ask him? That son of yours not only choose to mess with other people and this time he messed with Mr. Nathan who now wants to buy the Cross Group at a low price." With that Mr. Cross hanged up.

Mrs. Cross nearly fainted when she heard her husband's words.

Did this mean that the man who broke her son's leg was not an impersonator but the real Mr. Nathan? If that is it then...

She turned and looked at Rex who was looking at her whiles still cursing Mr. Nathan. "Mom you need to make him have a taste of how it feels like to have one's leg broken."

"Shut up!" Mrs. Cross yelled at Rex.

Huh?

"But mom..." Rex was stunned by his mother's sudden outburst.

Pointing a finger at him Mrs. cross yelled. "You..! Do you know what you have done?"

"Mom. I didn't do anything. He was the one who..."

"Shut up! Let me think of something."

"Mom. what are you talking about?" Rex was stunned. HIe didn't expect this reaction from his mom.

At that moment, he noticed that his mom's eyelids twitched nonstop and she was panting with a little bit of anger.

"I demand you to go look for Mr. Nathan and apologize immediately. If he doesn't forgive you, don't even think about coming back home." After that Mrs. Cross turned to walk away.

Rex was completely muddleheaded.

Clearly, it was him who had his leg broken, so why does he need to apologize to that impersonator? Or wait... He is really Mr. Nathan? No that's impossible.

Mr. Nathan wouldn't have taken a hillbilly like Hilda away

from him unless...

"Mom but why? I am the one with the broken leg!" Rex was confused as he yelled before his mom could walk out.

"Mr. Nathan was the one who broke your leg and now he is going after the Cross Group. We are going to be bankrupt soon if you don't look for him and apologize now.

Boom

Rex was dumbfounded as a bad hunch emerged in his mind. I am in trouble. He could feel his scalp bursting instantly.

He couldn't imagine what would become of him if Nathan really did take action against the Cross Group. "Mom, hurry.Use your connections to help me find Mr. Nathan before it is too late. You already know the kind of man he is."

Rex shuddered with a look of fear in his eyes as he spoke. I just messed with the ruthless devil, Nathan Clemon. Thinking about this scared Rex out of his wits.

Mrs. Cross and her son Rex were like ants on a hot pot as they urgently used their personal connections to make calls while trying to locate Mr. Nathan.

Rex hadn't seen this coming.
