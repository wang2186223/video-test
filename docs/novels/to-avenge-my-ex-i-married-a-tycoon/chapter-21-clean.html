Chapter 21

As Nathan heard them talking about his woman. His mouth curved slightly into a rare smile. "Yes. We are even married."

"What? Married!"

Both Samuel and Kyle were shocked.

Kyle was the first to recovered from the shock. "Bro, you are married?"

"Yes, I am married." Nathan replied calmly.

Samuel also asked. "Does your family approve of this? Don't you think it is a little too soon?"

Looking at him Nathan replied. "I don't need anyone's approval to get married."

Kyle shook his head before adding. "You know this isn't a best time to get married1. With all that's happening.."

"I know what I'm doing don't worry I got this." Nathan replied.

After chatting for a little while, Samuel and Kyle got up to leave. Before walking out of the house Samuel advised."You got to protect her from your uncle. You know he would use her to bring you down if he finds out about her."

Nodding Nathan saw them off. Immediately he closed the door.

his eyes turned cold. His uncle still hasn't learnt his lessons.

Taking out his phone, he made a call. "Execute the plan."

The next day.

Hilda woke up and yet again found herself in an unfamiliar room. When Nathan who was sitting by the window saw her open her eyes, he broke the silence. "You are awake." Nathan's voice was melodious as usual.

Hearing his voice, she turned and stared at him as she recalled everything. Her face blushed thinking about what happened between them last night. She had given herself to him last night. She would have to sleep with him anyway so it was all the same as she gave herself to him last night.

Nodding her head, Hilda spoke in a soft but hoarse voice."Hmm. I'm awake." Trying to get up from the bed, she felt sore all over.

Noticing this, Nathan poured her a jug of water before asking."You okay? After all last night, did I hurt you?"

Blushing the more, Hilda embarrassing answered. "I'm okay. I need to freshen up." With that she hurriedly went into the bathroom.

Watching her leave. Nathan smiled before heading out.

After a while. Hilda came out of the bathroom and noticed she didn't have any change of cloth. Opening the wardrobe. she noticed it was filled with men's shirts. Looking at herself in the bathrobe, she picked a random white shirt after all they were married and put it on before heading downstairs.

Although Nathan barely used this house, It was always attended to by a cleaner who passed by every once in a while to clean up and stock the fridge just in case.

As at now, Nathan was in the kitchen trying to make them something for breakfast. Walking into the hall, Hilda spotted Nathan in the kitchen. Looking at him who had an apron on and was busy, Hilda found him the more handsome.

Smiling, she thought to herself. 'It ain't bad marrying him.'

Sensing a gaze on him, Nathan turned around only to see Hilda staring at him tenderly whiles smiling foolishly.

Raising an eyebrow, Nathan smiled. "Like what you see?"

Snapping out of her daze Hilda awkwardly replied. "What?"

Knowing very well that she was embarrassed, Nathan didn't say anymore. "Just wait in the hall as the food would be ready soon."

Shaking her head Hilda walked up to him and asked."What are you making? Let me help."

"No. I'm almost done just go wait in the hall." Nathan said as he pushed Hilda out of the kitchen."

Still wanting to help Hilda refuted. "There is nothing for me to do in the hall just let me help you."

"No. I'm almost done. Just go and watch some TV." Nathan replied.

"Okay, Okay. Just tell me what you are making." Hilda decided to give in to him since he didn't want her to help him.

"Twice -baked potatoes, loaded with creamy mashed potatoes.sour cream, cheese and any topping you like. Just tell me the topping you like and I will served alongside some vegetables and soup." Nathan replied proudly and added. "Since you were a little exhausted, this simple home- made food would be able to nourish you and after that, we go and pack your stuffs to my other residence."

Listening to him, Hilda smiled but didn't notice the awkward situation they were in. As Nathan was pushing her out of the kitchen and she was also trying her best to see what he was preparing, they were clueless as to how intimate they were at that moment.

"The addition of crispy, crumbled bacon would take these

perfect twice-baked potatoes to the next level. How about that?"Hilda voiced out as she looked at Nathan.

Locking gazes, Nathan smiled as he stared at Hfilda.Noticing how Nathan was staring at her and how intimate they were.Hilda blushed before taking a few steps back. After that she run out of the kitchen. "I will be waiting in the hall."

Looking at her who was running out. Nathan smiled before shaking his head.

At the Adams residence, the Matriarch was fuming red as he heard that Hilda had harmed the young master of the Rex Family. Listening to his sons and grandsons rant on and on about how the Young Master was going to deal with them unless they send Hilda to his bed, she didn't know how to react.

She was beyond furious and just yelled. "That unfilial granddaughter! Someone get her here now!"

Mr. Lewis came forward and said. "Mother, don't work yourself up. I will make sure that that unfilial daughter of mine is sent to Mr. Rex's bed tonight don't worry."

"How can I not worry? Eh? Tell me!" Staring at Mr. Lewis who was standing in front of her, the Matriarch got the more angry."You couldn't even take care of a small issue as when she came to my birthday celebration and you think you can now? Hurry

up and call her up here."

Hesitating. Mr. Lewis finally said with his lowered. "But Hilda can't be found anywhere at the moment. After she escaped last night I sent people after her but they couldn't find her and she didn't go back to her apartment last night. We also tried reaching her on her phone but it is off."

"You loser! You can't even take care of your own daughter but you want to manage the company's affair. Dream on." The Matriarch said cruelly before turning to look at Michael her other son. "Michael, hurry up and track that unfilial granddaughter and ask her to get back here as soon as possible.11

At this time in Nathan's residence, the dishes were already served as Hilda and Nathan were eating silently on the dinning table.

Nathan was the first to break the silence. "How is it?"

"Hmm." With food in her mouth Hilda replied before looking up at him. "I love it!"
