Chapter 48

Reese smiled lightly. "Everyone has their own methods. If there are too many people, it's actually difficult to make decisions. Since he is gradually improving now, you don't have to worry."

"With your remarkable medical skills, I am sure I'm not needed. It's just that Malcolm, as a person,is reserved and somewhat cold. Please forgive him if his words make you uncomfortable."

Vincent gave the impression of a modest and caring gentleman. His way of speaking was also mild, which indeed contrasted greatly with Malcolm's personality.

However, they hadn't seen the tender side hidden within Malcolm, which could make one drown in it with just one look. Like today, facing so many people from the Flynn family. he still stood up for his wife. This touched her.

For so many years, besides her grandmother, no one had fought for her dignity and innocence.

Suddenly, someone stood up and spoke up for her,regardless of everything, and her icy heart seemed to warm up.

She raised her head, looking proud as she said,"A husband and wife are one. Besides,my beloved husband is very good to me. Yesterday, at the banquet,he bought me a dress designed by an Italian designer,the only one in the world. He would never say anything hurtful to me."

Reese said this with great certainty.

Vincent was genuinely stunned, especially by the overwhelming sense of happiness displayed on Reese's face. They hadn't been married for long,according to what Everly said, and Malcolm wasn't very fond of this girl before.

How did things suddenly change? They looked like a loving couple.

He forced a smile, tugging at the corners of his mouth."I'm relieved to see that you and Malcolm get along so well. I'll leave Malcolm's matters to you from now on."

"Of course, of course," Reese mimicked his fake sincerity. It was all just acting and who couldn't do that?

At that moment, Vincent's assistant, Darwin,

approached and whispered something in his ear.

Vincent's eyes dimmed for a brief moment,then he politely addressed Reese in front of him.

"Tll take my leave then. I have something to attend to in my room."

"Take care."

As Vincent walked away, the smile vanished from Reese's face, replaced by a disdainful snort.

"Don't think I can't see through your little scheme,"she scoffed.

He just wanted to fish information out of her, probe her secrets. If she was really that easy to expose,would she still be alive and marrying into the Flynn family?

Vincent and Darwin returned to the room and locked the door behind them.

"Did you find anything?"

"I did, but this woman's information was not easy to dig up. I had to go through several channels to find even a little bit."

Darwin handed the information to Vincent, who glanced at it briefly.

The earlier part was a simple resume, stating that she had been studying at a certain school since the age of 15, but it didn't specify the location.

Vincent didn't bother looking at the rest. He simply took out a lighter, burned the papers, and threw them into the ashtray. Darwin was puzzled as he asked, "Could it be that we're overthinking it? Maybe she's just an ordinary person, coming from backwoods with no special skills."

Vincent's eyes turned icy. "If she really hadno skills,could she make Malcolm, who was paralyzed,almost stand up again?"

Surprised, Darwin asked, "How is that possible? Mr.Vincent, weren't you always giving Master medication that should have worsened his condition over time?"

"That's why I find this woman suspicious. Just now, in Malcolm's room, she was able to determine the pharmacy from a medication package, and if it hadn't been for my foolish sister, she would have revealed the batch number as well. I even doubt she can recite every single word written on the package." This time, the Flynn family had married an extraordinary daughter-in-law. They couldn't even find her true identity, let alone know of her abilities.

Darwin emptied the burning ashes from the ashtray into the toilet and flushed it before returning.

"What should we do now? This woman has ruined your plan, and we can't proceed as originally planned."

"Continue investigating. We must find out everything about this woman's background for me." Otherwise,she might become their downfall.

"T'll go and find someone to continue the investigation," Darwin said without delay,hastily opening the door and leaving.

Before Reese went back to her room from the backyard, she made a phone call to Calvin, who was busy at the moment.

"What can I do for you?" Calvin asked.

"I need you to check Alyssa's whereabouts these past couple of days, especially when she went to the pharmacy. Make sure to investigate thoroughly and send the information to my phone, down to the exact time," Reese instructed.

Calvin didn't understand and asked, "What's the purpose of this investigation?"

"It's important. HIurry up and do it," Reese replied.

"Alright, I haven't even finished this game yet."

Reese ended the call and headed towards her room. Just as she was about to go up the stairs, Calvin sent a message.

After reading the message, Reese deleted it and a cunning smile flashed across her eyes.

Passing by Everly's room, she happened to overhear Alyssa complaining to Everly.

"That girl really knows how to hide her true colors.Despite being so good-looking,she deliberately disguises herself to deceive us. You should have seen her face today, it almost knocked me unconscious. It's on a goddess level."

Although Alyssa genuinely disliked Reese, she couldn't help but admit that among them, Reese had one of the highest levels of attractiveness which even rare in entire Atlanta. She rarely came across anyone with the elegant demeanor as Reese's. She looked at Everly and asked, "What kind of skincare products is she using? Or is it natural? How could she be so fair? Did you not notice how her whole body practically glows?"

However, Everly didn't seem to care about these things. Her expression was serious, and her eyes were sharp.

"Don't you think Reese is suspicious?" Everly asked.

Alyssa touched her chin. "Is it because she's too beautiful?"

Everly gave Alyssa a speechless look and mocked,"Are you stupid? Of course, it's because of her identty.It was said that she was raised in the Backwoods since she was little. For so many years, her father never showed any care towards her. Her life couldn't have been easy in Backwoods. Haven't you heard about Backwoods? People work tirelessly there every day.Can hard labor give you such flawless skin?"

She continued: "And wasn't it said that she never studied anything? Even if she did, she wouldn't have such extensive knowledge in the medical field. Just by looking at the packaging, she could figure out where your medicine was bought from."

Alyssa suddenly remembered. "Right, this girl is quite skilled. Could she be someone important?"

"I'm not someone important, and there's no need for you to speculate so hard here," Reese interrupted.

Suddenly, Reese pushed open the door and confidently walked in, without a hint of embarrassment or guilt for eavesdropping outside the door.

It startled Alyssa and Everly. Alyssa didn't know why she felt so nervous and shouted at Reese, "Who gave you the right to barge into someone's room? Didn't your parents teach you any manners?"

"Square,huh?"
