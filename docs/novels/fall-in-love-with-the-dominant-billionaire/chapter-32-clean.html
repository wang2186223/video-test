Everly watched her leave, a smug grin on her face.'After tonight, let's see how cocky you are.'

Did she really think the contract was a walk in the park?

Reese didn't sweat the contract at all. She'd closed bilion-dollar deals before; this small project was nothing. As long as the other party was serious, it was a breeze. If they were just playing games, not even Malcolm could help. When she got to Tranquil Haven Hotel, she immediately spotted a guy in his forties with a few strands of hair.overweight, especially his belly, which jiggledf as he walked.

Mack Woods squinted his beady eyes and gave Reese a once-over, clearly unimpressed.

Reese could tell he wasn't thrilled with her appearance, but she didn't care. That was the plan-to avoid any unwanted attention.

But she underestimated just how sleazy this guy was.

"If there's no problem, please sign the contract quickly."

But Mack didn't catch on to Reese's impatience, thinking she was just playing hard to get. "Ms. Brooks, what's the rush? I wasn't even gonna sign this project originally. But for you... Since I'm being so

As he spoke, Mack moved around the table and sat next to Reese,his hand reaching for her shoulder.

That crossed the line for Reese. Her eyes turned icy, and she twisted Mack's wrist, making him scream in the lobby.

Mack's face went pale, cold sweat dripping from his forehead in pain.

"You... let go of me, you bitch. It's your honor that I fancy you,and you dare to hurt me!"

From the moment Reese acted, her patience was gone.Her sharp eyes showed a killing intent that made Mack feel a twinge of fear.

How could an ordinary girl have such a look in her eyes?

She scoffed disdainfully. "Who'd want a greasy slob like you?Get a grip. If you dare touch me, you better be ready for the fallout!"

Even Malcolm hadn't laidI a hand on her, let alone this disgusting creep. Just looking at his greasy face was enough to make her sick.

The commotion drew a crowwd of onlookers.

"You crazy woman, let go of me! If I tell Mr.Flynn,you'll be fired. Look at yourself, so ugly. If it weren't for your good figure, I'd be disgusted just seeing you."

Reese sneered, "Oh yeah? If you find me disgusting, maybe Mr. Woods doesn't need these eyes anymore."

She grabbed a dessert knife and fork from the table, flipping them with lightning speed and aiming them at Mack's eyes Mack froze in fear, and the crowd gasped.

Just as the knife and fork were about to pierce Mack's pupils,Reese stopped, holding the utensils inches from his face.

Even so, Mack was so scared his legs gave out, and he collapsed to the ground in a mess.

Reese scoffed contemptuously. "You don't even have te guts,yet you dare to harass women."

At that moment, Mack's assistant came down from upstairs after arranging the room and saw Mack on the ground,pale with fear. He quickly approached.

"Mr. Woods, what happened to you?" Seeing someone finally come to his side. Mack felt a bit safer.

There was still fear in his eyes as he pointed at Reese.

"That... that woman, call Mr. Flynn immediately!"

The assistant didn't know what had happened but had to follow Mack's orders.

Malcolm was still in a meeting when Justin rushed in.

"Mr. Flynn, Reese is in trouble."

Malcolm frowned, his sharp gaze sweeping over the curious shareholders, making them lower their heads immediately.

"So, about this project,you..."

He wheeled himself over, his narrow eyes slightly squinting.a dangerous aura flashing across his handsome face.

"Come with me."

Justin nodded, "Yes,sir."

At the door of the conference room, Malcolm asked,"What happened?"

"Reese went to discuss the contract, but she got into a fight with Mr. Woods and even hit him."

Malcolm's throat tightened, a deep darkness flashing in his narrow eyes.

"Who sent her? Isn't this Everly's doing?"

"It seems Reese went out during the day, which made Miss Flynn unhappy. When Reese came back, Miss Flynn sent her to deliver the documents and sign the contract." Malcolm was furious. "She knew Mack was a lecherous

scoundrel and still sent Reese."

Justin rarely saw Malcolm so angry. The air around him seemed to thin, and he couldn't help but shiver.

"So,Mr.Flynn, what should we do now?"

"Let's go there."

"Dismiss the meeting. Is the project more important than my wife?"

Justin was shocked by his words.

What happened to Malcolm? Why was he teating Reese better and better? Was it because she was so different that he was getting used to her?

You bitch, just wait. I'll make sure you can't stay here!"
