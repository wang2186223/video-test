Chapter 100

"What's happening? Why do I feel like those two cars are ganging up on the one in the middle?"

"Who's in the middle? Looks like it's James from the Smith family."

"I can't see the faces of the drivers on either side. Who are they to be so bold? Aren't they worried about Mr. Smith's retaliation?"

The spectators were wide-eyed.chattering away.

"Is that the point? Look at that turn! Such skill! Not something just anyone can pull off."

Suddenly.someone shouted.

Immediately,people started standing up in excitement.

One person standing up inspired more people to stand up in excitement.

"Mr. Smith got left behind! Who would've thought we'd see this in today's race? Who are No. 7 and No. 8? They're amazing! Who do you think will win?"

"Doesn't that technique look familiar to anyone? I remember QS using it last year when he burst onto the scene!"

"Which one is QS? Maybe someone learned it from him. QS has been off the radar."

"Haven't you seen Twitter? He announced his comeback. Could it really be him?"

"Then who's the other one?"

Emily was unaware of the discussions.

After leaving James behind, she made a sharp turn.

For the first time, she encountered someone who could match her skills.igniting her competitive spirit.

When the opponent used the same technique. her eyes lit up.

Charles was also watching with excitement.

One was Daniel, and the other he recognized as QS through their technique.

His palms were sweaty with tension.

"Go number 7! Go number 8!"

Charles shouted.

His acquaintances turned to him. puzzled.

"Mr. Green, who are you rooting for?"

Charles snorted, "I'm happy no matter who wins!"

As he spoke. Emily's car edged past Daniel's, crossing the finish line first.

She knew the opponent had let her win a bit.

Otherwise,they would have tied for first place.

Regardless of the opponent's reasons, she won.

The twenty million dollars would fund her next experiment.

Emily got out of the car. nodded to the opponent. and said. "Thank you!"

Then she quickly prepared to leave.

She didn't want to reveal her identity yet.

But she didn't expect James to block her path after quitting the race.

"Who are you?"

He was looking for trouble!

Emily raised an eyebrow. mocking. "Mr. Smith. lost and now seeking revenge?"

She tried to walk past him.

"Don't go!"

James blocked her with his arm. "You and number 8 teamed up against me. I need to know who you are."

Emily looked at him with disgust, "What if I don't tell you? If you do anything, tomorrow's news will be #Mr. James Smith can't handle losing a race and abuses his power!"

"Tll ask you again, who are you? My patience is limited. Don't blame me if I get rough. Do you think your news will get our?"

James ignored Emily's words. Since she embarrassed him. she should be prepared for his retaliation.

"James.you've got some nerve!"

Daniel didn't want to reveal his identity. but Charles saw his low mood.

Charles could speak up.

Chatles smirked, his tone mocking. But James had to take him seriously.

"Charles Green!"

James' face changed. Charles was close to DDaniel and from the Green family. He couldn't afford to offend him. He noticed Daniel in his racing uniform next to Charles. So, these two targeted him on purpose. He didn't believe it was a coincidence.

"Calling my name won't help. You should leave before you embarrass yourself and the Smith family. You don't want Daniel to find out about today.do you?"

Charles glanced at Daniel as he spoke. Emily didn't notice, but she realized Charles' sharp tongue wasn't just for her; it was indiscriminate.

Seeing James silenced by Charles, she couldn't help but laugh. The sound was a clear provocation to James.

James thought, even if Charles intervened, he couldn't stop him. Charles wouldn't target him over this. He had the Smith family backing him.He needed to vent his anger.

"Mr. Green, you saw Emily provoke me. And the man next to her is clearly her accomplice. If they hadn't targeted me, I wouldn't have lost."

James believed his loss was due to their collusion.

Emily laughed louder, "Do you know what you look like right now?"

"What?" James didn't understand. But he knew Emily wouldn't say anything nice.

"Idiot!"

Daniel spoke. He lowered his voice, making it hard for Emily to recognize.

"Someone, I suspect these two of cheating for the twenty million dollars.

The man clearly let the woman win!"

James' face was dark. Especially now, spectators wanting to meet Emily gathered around. Most participants had some wealth. They were in the same circle, and seeing James like this, they thought he couldn't handle losing.

Someone whispered. "What's going on? Can't handle losing?"

"He thinks they targeted him. If number 7 and number 8 hadn't teamed up,he wouldn't have lost."

"How can he say that? The final turn, their skills aren't something just anyone can do!"

James heard the murmurs. HIis face was grim. "Shut up! Who told you to talk?"

"Tell me.who are you?"

James's shout silenced everyone. He turned to Emily.

Emily stepped forward aggressively, "You want to know who I am? What will you do then?"

Daniel watched Emily's expression and demeanor,unable to suppress his smile.

"Yes. I'm curious too. Since you think we're accomplices, what will you do?

.

Though Daniel lowered his voice, his tone was unchanged. Speaking so much at once, Emily found his voice familiar. She couldn't help but look at him more closely.
