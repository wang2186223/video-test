Chapter 39

"Emily, hold up a sec!"

Emily saw Sophia leave and was about to head straight towards the person lurking in the shadows.

Suddenly,someone called out to her, panting heavily.

Emily recognized the voice, her expression turning icy, and kept walking without acknowledging it.

The person quickly stepped in front of Emily, blocking her path. "You heard me, why won't you stop?"

As she spoke, she stomped her foot, almost like she was throwing a tantrum.

Emily frowned, her eyes narrowing as she looked at her, as if to say, "Are you out of your mind?"

What's wrong with her? Acting cute to the person she didn't get along with?

"Emily,I'm sorry!"

Lily understood Emily's look, her face turning red with slight embarrassment.

But she bit her lip and mustered the courage to say it.

Emily's frown deepened,becoming more alert. "What kind of trick are you and Bianca planning now?Pretending to be nice to me and then stabbing me in the back?"

Lily's expression froze.

She hadn't expected her attempt at reconciliation to be misunderstood in this way. She was also pampered at home, and now she was being misunderstood even after admitting her mistake.

Lily's expression turned colder, and she huffed, "I don't have as many schemes as you. I came to apologize for misunderstanding you about Bianca's near accident last time."

Emily crossed her arms, looking at her leisurely. "I remember you were yelling at me in the office just now.

This sudden change makes me suspicious, doesn't it?"

Lily was momentarily speechless.

She pursed her lips and sincerely apologized, "I just saw the photos and videos and realized I misunderstood you. It was my mistake, and I apologize. But that doesn't mean you weren't wrong for bullying Bianca before.

Bianca is kind-hearted. If you just got along with her, you could be the best of friends."

Emily had always thought of Lily as Bianca's most loyal follower, and since she disliked Bianca, she also disliked Lily.

She hadn't expected Lily to have some sincerity.

It seemed like she was just another fool deceived by Bianca.

"I accept your apology. And for the record, it's never been me trying to make things difficult for her. That line won't work on me."

Emily said, then walked past Lily and left.

"...What does that mean?"

Lily was momentarily confused.

She reached out, wanting to stop Emily and ask for clarification. "Lily,why did you run off alone? Who were you talking to just now?" Bianca, realizing something was off with Lily, hurriedly chased after her with a bad feeling.

But she only saw Lily standing there, staring into the distance.

The figure walking away seemed to be Emily.

She quickly approached and grabbed Lily, asking.

"I just found out I misunderstood Emily, so I thought I should apologize and persuade her to get along with you."

Lily didn't hide anything and replied honestly.

Bianca's heart sank.

Lily, who had always disliked Emily, was starting to change her mind over one incident. This couldn't be happening.

Bianca's eyes reddened, feeling guilty."It's all my fault for being unwell these days and not knowing about the online stuff. By the time I found out and wanted to explain, it was too late. Emily probably thinks I did it and hates me even more."

Lily thought for a moment but didn't follow Bianca's lead. Instead, she advised, "Don't be sad. Maybe Emily didn't think that much. She probably isn't that petty. Maybe we misunderstood her. She accepted my apology just now."

"And when I suggested she get along with you, guess what she said? She said, 'It's never been me trying to make things difficult for her. I should be advising you."

"What does that mean? So, you think I'm the one making things difficult for her?" Bianca's face turned cold, unable to control her outburst.

She had always been gentle and soft-spoken.

This cold and hostile demeanor was something Lily had never seen before.

"I-I didn't mean that. Bianca, I just thought maybe you're too sensitive and misunderstood her hostility towards you."

Lily was scared, her voice trembling.

She even instinctively distanced herself from Bianca.

Bianca had been facing setbacks today, something she had never experienced before.

She wasn't naturally good-tempered, so she couldn't control her emotions and revealed her true feelings.

"Sorry, I was just afraid you'd think I'm a bad person. You know you're the friend I care about the most,and I'm scared of losing you, so I got so worked up."

Bianca quickly explained.

Lily,having been friends with her since childhood, immediately believed her.

She was deeply moved, seeing Bianca on the verge of tears, and hugged her, patting her back to comfort her.

"I know. I just want you two to get along so you don't have to be sad and sick every time."

"Thank you for understanding, Lily!" Bianca hugged her, her voice choked with emotion.

But her eyes, hidden from Lily's view, were cold and calculating.

Emily was becoming harder to deal with. What had happened to her recently?

Bianca thought of the phone call Emily made to Daniel. No matter what, she had to go and see for herself.

She hid her dark thoughts and released Lily.

Then she said. "Lily, wait for me. I'll go talkto Emily and let her know I genuinely want to get along."

"Okay, I'll wait for you!" Lily agreed without much thought.

Bianca let go of Lily and hurried to catch up with Emily.

Emily was almost at the hiding spot when her phone rang

"Mr. Smith, are you here?"

"I'm already at the school gate, by the roadside."

Daniel had seen Emily and thought she hadn't noticed his car, so he called to remind her.

As he spoke, he gave the driver a look.

The driver understood and rolled down the window.

Emily heard the call and turned to look at the roadside.

She saw a Maybach with its hazard lights on, signaling her.

The window rolled down.

Emily and Daniel locked eyes from a distance. His eyes were deep and dark, and when their gazes met, it felt like he was drawing her in completely.

Daniel!

Mysterious and intimidating.

Emily thought of the danger lurking nearby.

Daniel meant well, but she didn't want to put him in danger.

She stepped forward but didn't open the car door. Instead, she leaned on the window and looked at Daniel inside.

Her face, hidden in the shadows, added to the mystery.

"Mr. Smith, I'm sorry. Something came up, and I might be late. Could you give me an address, and I'11 come over later?"

Emily apologized.

Daniel's face, hidden in the shadows, was hard to read.

"Alright."

After a long pause.

Just when Emily thought he might be angry about her delay, she heard his concise reply.

Emily breathed a sigh of relief.

"See you later, Mr. Smith!" Emily waved and stepped back, turning to walk away.

At that moment, Bianca had already caught up.

She was about to call out to Emily, but saw her talking to someone in the Maybach and then turning to leave.

As Emily moved aside. the window slowly rolled up, and Bianca saw the cold, distant face inside.

Bianca's pupils contracted, her eyes widening.

How could it be him?
