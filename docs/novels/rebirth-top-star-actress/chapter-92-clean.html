Chapter 92

"Emily. I didn't expect you to follow me here. Have you finally realized your mistake?"

James nodded in agreement with Bianca, thinking Emily had come to the racetrack just for him. After all. Emily had never been to such a place before. so how could she have found it otherwise?

Emily acted like she hadn't heard their conversation and said directly,"Excuse me, you're blocking the way. Can you move?"

She spoke and then tried to walk past them toward the distance.

"Emily,James was only upset with you because of your past behavior. He's still your fiancé. Since you've come all this way, just apologize and make up with him," Bianca lectured.

James raised an eyebrow, looking down at Emily with a smug expression.waiting for her to apologize.

"Who said I came here for him? Can you stop being so full of yourselves?"Emily rolled her eyes at them and continued walking.

James's face darkened. He instinctively reached out to grab Emily. "Emily,since you followed me here, why are you still pretending? This time,I'll be generous and won't call off the engagement, considering our past. Just stay by my side, and we'll talk after the race."

Emily sidestepped to avoid James's hand, but someone else was quicker-a hand slapped James's away.

"What are you doing? Get your dirty hands off her! Ms. Johnson isn't someone you can touch so casually." Byron, who had been chatting with some racing buddies and was delayed, arrived just in time to see the scene.Without even looking at James's face, he defended Emily and slapped James's hand away.

Byron quickly approached and checked on Emily. "Ms. Johnson, are you okay? Don't worry. I'll handle this."

He then turned to James, ready to unleash his anger, but when he saw James's face, he paused and then smiled lazily. "Well, if it isn't Mr. Smith.Didn't you see that Ms. Johnson doesn't want to talk to you? Why are you still pushing it? You need to respect women, you know? Besides..."

He deliberately glanced at Bianca with a suggestive smile. "You already have another lady friend, don't you? Just focus on her."

Byron couldn't understand why he had once thought Bianca was great.Emily had been right; he must have been blind. Bianca's pretentiousness was now clear to him, and he found Emily's straightforwardness and boldness much more appealing.

Bianca sensed Byron's disdain and was furious. She almost ground her molars to dust. Byron had once been her admirer, but because of Emily,his attitude had changed drastically. She couldn't understand what Emily had done or said to him.

"Byron, what a coincidence you're here too. Don't misunderstand; it's not James trying to trouble Emily. Emily came here specifically for James,but she's shy and embarrassed, which is why you saw what you did," Bianca explained,tilting her head to show her best side and speaking in what she thought was a gentle and reasonable tone.

"Nonsense!" Byron spoat, clearly disgusted by her words. "Who said Ms.Johnson came for Mr. Smith? I personally invited her here. You must be mistaken. I have a race soon, and I want Ms. Johnson to see me in action.so I won't waste time talking to you."

Byron turned to Emily, his expression softening instantly. "Ms. Johnson.sorry for being late. Let's go. I'll show you my car first, and then you can ride shotgun and experience it before the race."

Emily was increasingly pleased with Byron's attitude. She nodded, "Sure!"

"Emily!" James couldn't believe Emily was really leaving. She wasn't here for him? He couldn't accept it, especially seeing her with Byron, whom he considered a lowlife. Emily must have been led astray by such people.

"Are you really going to stick wwith these shady characters? This is my last warning. If you don't come to your senses, I'll publicly call off the engagement. No one will treat you well because you're my fiancée anymore!"

Emily was infuriated by his words and stopped in her tracks. Bianca's eyes narrowed. hoping Emily would leave so she could become James's wife.

James sneered inwardly. Emily must be reluctant to give up her status as his fiancée.

Emily laughed coldly. "Treated well as your fiancée? You mean being pushed into the water, locked in the bathroom, or thrown down the stairs?I can't take any more of that 'kindness.' Go ahead and announce the breakup!"

"What? Ms. Johnson, you've been through that? That's outrageous! Who

dared to bully you? Tell me, and I'll make sure they pay!" Byron was puzzled. Emily was so strong; how could she tolerate such treatment? It must be the power of love. James, as her fiancé, hadn't protected her.so it was his fault.

Byron glared at James. "Mr. Smith, if you can't protect your woman,you should let her go. From now on. Ms. Johnson has nothing to do with you.I'll take care of her!"

Byron then escorted Emily away.

James was left stunned by Emily's words. Had she really suffered so much without him knowing? Why hadn't she ever told him? All he had heard was that Emily was jealous of Bianca and constantly schemed against her.Bianca had always pitied Emily and indulged her.

"Bianca, is what Emily said true?" James asked urgently, his brow furrowed.

Bianca was startled by his sudden anger. Her heart sank, but she forced tears and said helplessly, "James, I don't know! Emily never told me. She's not one to be weak or tolerant. Maybe she said that to make you feel sorry for her?"

James frowned, but he was still somewhat swayed.

Bianca continued, "If Emily lied to make you feel sorry, it means she still cares about you, right? That's good. It means you can reconcile with her.No matter what she does, it's because she loves you. Just coax her, and she'Il become obedient again."

James frowned, contemplating Bianca's words.

At that moment, the broadcast announced the start of the first race, calling

the participants to their positions.
