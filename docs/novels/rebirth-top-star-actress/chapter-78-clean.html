Chapter 78

"It's about Ms. Johnson!"

Jace heard the raspy, menacing voice. He figured he had interrupted James at a bad time.

He wiped imaginary sweat from his forehead and braced himself.

James's face turned cold, his interest vanishing instantly. He got out of bed and went to his study to take the call.

Bianca, frustrated and unsatisfied, watched James leave without a second thought,nearly grinding her teeth in anger.

She had heard Emily's name.

Her anger towards Emily was reaching its peak.

That wretch! If she had known Emily would turn out like this, she would have taken measures to get rid of her as soon as she returned.

In the study.

James's face was stern. "Speak."

"Ms. Johnson's information seems to be encrypted. There's even a highly skilled tech expert involved. During my investigation, my computer got hacked. Luckily, they only wanted to teach me a lesson and formatted one of my files."

Jace was still a bit shaken as he spoke.

His computer contained another very important bid document with confidential data.

James naturally thought of this, his brows furrowing.

"Then hire a private investigator. Don't get involved yourself!" James was still unwilling to give up.

Jace was helpless. He had been working with James for a long time and knew Emily was James's fiancée. He also knew how James treated Emily.

Now he was suddenly concerned. Where was this concern before?

But Jace could only think this to himself. He wouldn't dare say it to James's face unless he wanted to lose his job. James was known for being petty and vengeful.

However, someone took this opportunity to curry favor with Emily.

Terry Perez: [Boss, someone was investigating you. I gave them a little warning. It was James's assistant.]

Emily: [Good job. Actually, I have something I need you to look into.]

Emily hadn't expected James to be still so persistent, even sending Jace to investigate her.

Of course, she agreed with Winter's approach. Jace was just an employee;as long as it wasn't excessive, she wouldn't bother with it.

As for Terry, he was ranked second on the dark web.

The first was naturally her. Terry had challenged her once, but after she toyed with him, he insisted on becoming her follower.

In her previous life, she was focused on the Johnson family and had no interest in followers.

In this life, she wanted to strengthen herself and needed people to help her with these tasks.

Besides, Terry seemed quite reliable.

Terry: [Boss,you finally treat me as your follower. That's great.]

This meant Emily now considered him one of her own, and he might even get a chance to meet her.

Emily: [Help me find out the auction times for Golden Saffron Essence and Celestial Ginseng, including the organizers and participant list,and obtain an invitation for me.]

In her previous life, she had heard about these herbs being auctioned but didn't know the specifics.

Terry agreed readily.

After arranging everything, Emily also asked Terry to help her with AI programming.

No one was more suitable than Terry. She was too busy and might not have the time.

Since she wanted to confront the Johnson family, she needed to tackle them one by one.

She had to develop AI technology before Aiden.

Emily finished her arrangements and received a call from her friend Wren Kelly.

Wren was Chase's wife in her previous life.

Wren told her she was participating in the medical competition.

Emily wasn't sure if Wren had participated in her previous life. She owed Wren a lot and planned to meet her to make amends by teaching her medical knowledge.

The next day.

Emily arrived at the venue and met Wren.

Wren's face was full of youthful glow, her dimples making her look both beautiful and adorable when she smiled.

When Emily mentioned teaching her medical skills. Wren was so excited she almost squealed. "That's awesome!"

"Shh!" Emily reminded her to keep it down.

Not many people knew about Emily's medical prowess, but Wren did.They had met on a mountain where Wren witnessed Emily's life-saving skills.

Wren immediately made a zipping motion with her mouth.

But their commotion caught Bianca's attention.

She glanced over and saw Emily's profile. "No way, it can't be her."

Bianca shook her head, immediately dismissing the thought.

James looked at her, puzzled. "What's up?"

Because they had been intimate again last night, he had come along today,considering her condition. Seeing her reaction, he thought she might be unwell.

Bianca wanted to shake her head. "It's nothing. I must have been mistaken."

"Emily!" James followed her gaze and saw Emily turning her head. He almost spat out her name through gritted teeth.

Bianca didn't have time to stop him as he rushed over to Emily.

He grabbed her. "How did you get in here? This place isn't for someone who only knows a few herbs from the countryside. Come with me!"

This time, he had caught her and intended to question her about her recent activities.

James was accompanied by his assistant, Milo, who had been assigned by the event organizer, considering James's status in the Smith family.

Emily suddenly grabbed and twisted his hand in return.

"Emily, Emily, let go! How can you treat James like this? He's only concerned for you. You didn't register for the competition. How did you sneak in? This won't do,let go of James and leave!"

Bianca stepped forward to help but retreated under Emily's cold gaze.resorting to verbal accusations.

Their commotion drew the attention of those around them.

Bianca felt emboldened and continued, "Emily, this place values rules. You can't break them and make things difficult for James. Also, since you don't have a spot, who helped you sneak in?"

"Emily, I didn't expect you to fall so low. Even if I have to use force, I'm taking you away. Someone, take her away!"

James waved his hand, acting with authority.

Emily's hand held a silver needle, her eyes alert.

If anyone approached, she wouldn't hesitate.

"Stop, I dare anyone to try!"

At that moment, a commanding voice rang out.

The crowd instinctively parted.

Emily looked up and met a pair of deep eyes. He was backlit,walking towards her.

It reminded her of the saying: My prince will appear to protect me just in time.

Though she could handle it herself, being defended felt nice.

But she quickly shook off those distracting thoughts.
