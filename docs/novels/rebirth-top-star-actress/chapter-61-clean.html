Chapter 61

"Chase, do you know her?" A wwoman lay on the hospital bed, her eyes dark and sunken, her body frail and thin.

Despite her condition, her bone structure and features revealed she was a beauty.

At this moment, hearing Chase's words, she struggled to lift her gaze and looked at Emily, asking.

"Of course, I know her. She's not some amazing doctor, just a daughter we found in the trash heap." Chase's cold expression immediately softened upon hearing Aurora Rivera's voice, and he patiently explained.

After explaining, he turned to Emily,′′Iknew you were restless, but I didn't expect you to turn out like this. I came back this time to teach you a lesson. You're coming home with me." His tone left no room for refusal.

In the past, Emily would have immediately apologized and obediently followed.

But now, Emily had no intention of complying

Her gaze was more focused on the weak person on the bed.

'So, this is the person Chase likes?

In her previous life, she didn't know about Aurora's existence at first. When her friend fell in love with Chase at first sight, she begged Emily to help.

Emily agreed to set them up.

If Chase had outright rejected her friend, it would have been fine.

But he accepted her friend's affection, even married her, only to become cold and abusive when Aurora returned.

1By the time Emily saved Hayden and returned to the country,her friend had already left in heartbreak and despair.

When Chase regretted it and wanted to win her back, it was too late.

She hadn't expected Chase and Aurora to repeat their history so soon.

If the problem was solely with Chase, if he was the one pursuing Aurora, Emily wouldn't blame Aurora.

But Aurora initially looked down on Chase for being a minor celebrity. Later, when Chase became a Best Actor and his identity as a member of the Johnson family was revealed, Aurora knew Chase had a wife but still maintained an ambiguous, flirtatious attitude towards him. That was her fault.

Thinking of this, Emily's expression grew even colder. "Chase, you should leave. I think Emily doesn't like me very much.She probably knows you're here to see me, so she pretended to be a doctor to try to kill me," Aurora said coldly, sensing Emily's displeasure.

Chase's gaze shot towards Emily.

He saw Emily looking coldly at Aurora, and his face darkened.

He stepped forward and forcefully grabbed Emily, "Who taught you to look at people like that? Apologize to Aurora right now, and then go back to the Johnson Villa and wait for me. You'd better listen,or don't blame me for being harsh when I get back."

Chase threatened coldly.

But as soon as his hand grabbed her wrist, Emily twisted his arm behind his back.

"...Emily, what are you doing? Let go of me, are you crazy?" Chase gritted his teeth, sweat forming on his forehead from the pain. Aurora was startled by Emily's sudden action but quickly regained her composure.

Pretending to be concerned, she said, "You don't need to show off for me. Chase being here is just a coincidence. If you don't want us to have any interaction, take him away. Whether I live or die has nothing to do with him. Take him away."

She turned to Floyd, "Mr. Ryan, we trusted your medical skills.Even if you can't treat me, you shouldn't introduce such a young girl. You heard Chase admit she doesn't know anything about medicine. This is fraud! It's risking lives! We're done here, Vida, let's go."

She decisively instructed her assistant, Vida.

"Yes, Ms. Rivera, I'll help youa!" Vida quickly stepped forward to assist her.

"Emily, let go of me!" Chase struggled, trying to break free from Emily. Emily suddenly released him, causing him to stumble.

His agent caught him just in time, preventing him from hitting the wall.

Freed, Chase hurried to catch up with Aurora.

"Aurora, don't go. This is all Emily's fault. I'll make sure she gets punished. Don't worry, if Mr. Ryan can't help, I'll find a way to cure you."

Chase's usual cold demeanor was replaced by the image of a cluamsy boy chasing his beloved.

Emily watched the scene unfold.

Aurora played hard to get, stepping back to avoid Chase's hand,her tone softening, "I appreciate your kindness. I know my condition well. Let's leave it to fate. I'll go now. Thank you for today."

She bit her lip and tried to walk out. Wait!"

Floyd, who had been silent, called out to Aurora.

Though Floyd couldn't cure Aurora, he was a respected figure in the medical field, surrounded by experts. Aurora couldn't afford to be rude.

Aurora, pale and frail, asked, "Mr. Ryan, is there something else?"

Floyd snorted,"Idon't mind you questioning my skills. I can't cure your illness, and I understand your resentment. But you shouldn't doubt Emily's abilities. Apologize, now!"

Aurora and Chase's faces changed.

Aurora's nails dug into Vida's flesh, but she refused to apologize,"Mr. Ryan, I don't know how she's deceived you, but I trust Chase.She doesn't know anything about medicine. I won't apologize."

"You..." Floyd was about to speak. Emily stepped forward.

She sneered at Aurora, "I don't want an insincere apology. But you'd better find a good doctor soon, or your body will start to rot."

"Emily, you're so vicious. How dare you curse Aurora? You need a lesson..."

Chase,furious,raised his hand to slap Emily.

Emily, now quick to react, wouldn't stand there and take it.

She struck first, slapping Chase.

"You dare hit me? Are you crazy?" Chase looked at Emily in disbelief.

As a celebrity, he always took care of his skin. Emily's slap instantly made his face swell. Feeling the pain, he tried to hit Emily, "I'll teach you a lesson today."

He thought the slap was an accident, that he hadn't reacted in time.

Emily dodged and took out her phone, recording his angry,disheveled state.

"What are you doing? You trash heap reject...

Chase,furious, spat out hurtful words.

Emily froze.

'They say a man's words in anger reveal his true thoughts.'

It turned out he never truly respected her. His previous kindness was just a facade.

Lost in thought, Emily didn't dodge Chase's slap, which landed on

her face.
