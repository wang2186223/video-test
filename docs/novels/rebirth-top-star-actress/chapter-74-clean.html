Chapter 74

"Bianca!" James pulled her into his arms, his voice full of concern. "What's going on?"

Bianca leaned against his chest, a calculating glint in her eyes.

"James. I'm okay. I was just really tired last night," she said, her cheeks flushing a deep red, like a sunset.

Her soft words carried a hint of ambiguity.

James's mind drifted back to the previous night's intimacy. Seeing her blushing face, he felt his throat go dry.

At his age, full of youthful vigor. Emily had always refused any premarital intimacy, making Bianca the first woman he had been with.

James's gaze deepened.

But considering Bianca's condition, he suppressed his thoughts.

Bianca could sense the change in James's body, the heat of his skin, and his heavy breathing, all of which indicated his desire.

A smile flickered in her eyes.

Having achieved her goal, she struggled to stand up, only to almost fall again. After a few attempts, she finally managed to stand, giving him a weak smile. "James, I'm sorry for worrying you again. I need to catch a flight to Emberfall City. Don't worry, I won't let this... interfere with important matters!"

She tried to walk out with determination.

Though James hadn't promised to take responsibility for her, his eyes were filled with growing affection and tenderness. He grabbed her wrist and then lifted her into his arms.

"You're too weak. I'll take you to Emberfall City!"

"But I..."

"I promised you. Don't refuse! Even if I can't give you a commitment, at least let me do something to make up for it!"

James spoke heavily.

Bianca met his determined gaze and softened, nodding. "Okay."

And so, James carried her to the car.

They returned to the Johnson Villa to grab some clothes before heading to the airport for Emberfall City.

The Johnson family had been busy these days. Eli was occupied with a company design project, Aiden was overwhelmed with AI matters,and Hayden was under the impression that he was negotiating a major project with a friend, who was currently planning a trip to Zephyria.

Finn was likely participating in some competition again.

Chase, who was supposed to be back, was busy taking Aurora to Emberfall City for medical treatment.

So,only Cleo was at home.

Cleo watched as Bianca and James returned to grab their things,looking at

them with a satisfied expression. Before Bianca left, she gave a special reminder, "James needs gentle companionship. Be understanding. unlike Emily. Men prefer gentle, girly women."

The implication was that Emily wasn't like a typical girl.

"Mom. I understand," Bianca said shyly, lowering her head.

Coincidentally, when they got off the plane, they checked into the same Azure Palace Hotel where Emily was staying.

Unexpectedly, they ran into Emily at the elevator as she was heading out to meet Aaron.

"Emily, what are you doing here?" Bianca asked in surprise.

James, who was supporting Bianca, instinctively pulled his hand back when he saw Emily.

Emily wasn't surprised to see them.

"I can be wherever I want. Is that a problem for you?"

She had noticed their intertwined hands, feeling a pang of sadness.

Had they already gotten together so soon?

Their auras were too obvious, filled with each other's presence.

And...

Emily glanced at Bianca's neck.

Bianca. perhaps intentionally, adjusted her collar to reveal the red marks on her neck more clearly.

Do vou have to taik like that? Don't pretend you don't care. You knew we were coming and followed us here. If you want me to value you. you should listen to what i have to say. What a narcissist!

He actually thought she came to Emberfall City for him.

Emily felt a wave of sarcasm.

Just then, she saw Aaron entering the hotel and didn't want to waste time with the self-absorbed pair.

"Narcissism is a disease. If you're sick. get treated. Don't disgust me!"

With that. she brushed past James and hurried towards Aaron.

Aaron had come because a pharmaceutical company wanted to collaborate with them. Hearing that Emily was in Emberfall City. he insisted on meeting her.

He was there to take Emily to the meeting.

"You..,"

"James, look!"

James,ignored by Emily, was naturally angry.

Bianca quickly grabbed James and pointed at Aaron, who was now greeting Emily with a relaxed smile.

James's face turned dark.

How long had it been since Emily smiled at him like that?

Now she was smiling like that at a man he didn't even know.

So. this was why she had been acting up lately. because of another man?

Without a word. Jamnes wanted to chase after them.

Bianca knew what he intended to do.

She stumbled, leaning softly into James's arms.

"Bianca..."

James's thoughts were pulled back, and he asked worriedly, "Are you feeling unwell again?"

"It's nothing. I probably didn't rest well, and the two-hour flight made me tired." Bianca said gently, shaking her head. She even offered, "James, you don't have to worry about me. If you want to chase after Emily,go ahead.She's young and might get deceived."

"If she gets deceived, it'sher own fault. She's been so disobedient lately. If something happens, let it be a lesson for her!"

James,feeling guilty about last night with Bianca, said this deliberately.

Bianca wanted to say more, but James was already firmly supporting her to go upstairs and rest.

However, even while taking care of Bianca, James's mind was elsewhere.

Bianca noticed this, her eyes turning cold.

James's brow furrowed tightly, as if he could crush a mosquito, but he couldn't just leave Bianca behind.

At that moment, his phone rang.

It was his assistant,Jace.

"Mr. Smith. Harold Rogers is in Emberfall City. He's here for a new pharmaceutical investment and will be participating in the upcoming medical competition."

Harold was the biggest investor in the pharmaceutical industry. His personal involvement demonstrated his strong commitment to this project.

James's Ascend Global Group needed such an investor for their transformation. This was an opportunity he couldn't miss.

He also wanted to prove himself to his father, Kade Smith, especially after his mother, Quiana, had made a huge mistake. He needed to outshine the adopted son, Jett Smith.

"Send me the address and his information."

After giving instructions, James found an excuse to tell Bianca, "Bianca,you rest well. I have to go out for a bit."
