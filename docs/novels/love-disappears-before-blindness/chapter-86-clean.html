Chapter 86

He stared at her intently, and a strong sense of hostility immediately erupted from his entire being.

Right now. Amelia looked so fragile, her face all pale.

She just leaned against the headboard, staring blankly out the window,a dried tear at the corner of her eye, like she'd given up on everything.

When she heard his words, she frowned instinctively, not sure if she was dreaming or if this was real. He was already by her side,sitting down and holding her cold hand.

"How could this happen, the baby..."

"Shut up!" Amelia snapped, and in that moment, all her anger,disappointment, and regret flared up. She instinctively blamed him.

He was the father, he should share the pain she was going through.

"Holly,get him out." Amelja's voice was raspy.

Chris's face kept changing, thinking she was mad at him for not using a condom during sex, leading to the damage to her body.

So, he tried to be patient and comfort her, "DDon't be like this, it'll pass. I'll get someone to help you recover, and if you want a child in the future..."

"I don't. Get out!" Amelia pushed him away and turned her back on him.

Holly stepped in, "Mr. Spencer, Ms. White isn't in a good mood right now,maybe you should go out and rest for a bit."

As soon as she said that, Chris shot her a cold look, and Holly shivered,

walking out with two nurses quitely.

Now it was just the two of them in the big ward.

"It's my fault." Chris sighed, looking at her with a mix of emotions.

They'd been married for years without a kid, and she'd faced all kinds of gossip because of it.

But now, they had a child only to lose it. That kind of pain was huge,no wonder she couldn't handle it.

"Didn't you hear me? I told you to leave! Besides, the child isn't yours.why are you blaming yourself?" Amelia sneered, looking at him like he was an idiot.

Chris was furious, "Don't joke about this!"

Amelia was pleased with his anger, and said slowly, "I'm telling the truth,are you scared to hear it?"

Chris stared at her for a long time, holding back his anger, and then left the ward.

He went to find the doctor and learned that the baby in Amelia's belly was less than a month old and that an ectopic pregnancy had caused her to bleed heavily and almost die. His anger faded.

After all, he was partly to blame for her suffering. But she was so stubborn.

"Mr, Spencer, Ms. White's mental state has been poor these past few days,please don't provoke her." Holly's voice was almost pleading.

Chris glanced at her, "Is there any way to make her feel better?"

Holly thought for a moment, "MMs. White hasn't had much of an appetite lately and rarely eats. If you can get her to eat something, it would really help her body."

Chris waved his hand, "Got it."

That afternoon, Chris walked into the kitchen for the first time, shocking everyone.

"Teach me a simple, easy-to-make, delicious, and nutritious dish," he demanded.

The five-star chefs in the kitchen were all stunned.

They looked at each other, all wanting to bolt.

Not to mention anything else, just considering Chris's crazy request and his zero knowledge of cooking, it seemed almost impossible to make something edible.

The kitchen went silent.

Chris grew impatient, his face darkening, "What, is this too hard for you guys?"

He snorted, his presence a bit intimidating.

The head chef, Fabian Bowen, suddenly had an idea, "There is a dish that s simple, easy to learn, delicious, and nutritious."

Chris raised an eyebrow, "What is it?"

Fabian beamed,"Spaghetti with tomato sauce."

"How to cook< it?" Chris asked.

Fabian quickly grabbed a tablet and explained, "You cook the spaghetti until it's done, drain it, then make the tomato sauce, mix it with the spaghetti, sprinkle some cheese on top, andI you have a nutritious and delicious dish."

Chris frowned, "That's it? Sounds pretty basic."

Fabian rolled his eyes internally, thinking, 'The point is, you can't handle anything more complicated.'

Holly quickly chimed in, "Mr. Spencer, spaghetti with tomato sauce is not only easy to make and delicious, but it's also easy to digest and won't burden a weak body. Plus, we have to consider your ability to complete it,right?"

He frowned deeply, "What do you mean? You think I can't do it?"

Holly was at a loss for words and lowered her head awkwardly.

Soon, Chris signaled the others to help him get started with the spaghetti.They first arranged an apron for Chris and put on gloves to avoid him cutting his hand while slicing the tomatoes. They even washed the tomatoes and took out the cheese.

Chris disdainfully took off the gloves, "Wearing gloves to cut a tomato?You guys are so delicate."

Fabian and the others exchanged glances.

It wasn't that they were delicate, it was that Mr. Spencer's fingers were delicate.

"Just chop the tomatoes finely," Fabian carefully instructed.

Chris started, the first cut was very successful, the second cut was barely acceptable, the third cut, the tomato rolled, and everyone gasped,but he wasn't hurt.

Chris grew impatient, and his pride and confidence were challenged.

It was just a damn tomato, how hard could it be to cut?

He used a bit of force, and as he cut down, he felt a pain in his left pinky.

When his blood started to flow, everyone panicked.

"Mr. Spencer's hand is injured!"

"Quick, get a bandage for Mr. Spencer."

"Let us handle it. Mr. Spencer, could you please take a break?"

Everyone was in chaos.

Chris was extremely annoyed and told everyone to leave,leaving only Fabian.

After much effort and four failed attempts, the spaghetti with tomato sauce was finally barely presentable.

Chris personally brought the spaghetti to the ward.

Before this, Holly had repeatedly reminded Amelia to avoid the negative comments, as Chris had spent the entire afternoon making it, and she should at least take a few bites and not give it a bad review.

Amelia was momentarily stunned.

He actually made food for her in person.

He probably didn't even know where the stove switch was.

A plate of spaghetti with tomato sauce was quickly placed in frot of her.

Chris's expression was aloof, "Just something I threw together, try it."

Amelia thought, she still needed to get his sperm in the future, so she couldn't completely ignore him now.

She had to suppress her emotions and reluctantly took it, taking a bite.

The first bite was a bit sour, but after eating it, there was a slight sweetness,the noodles were a bit soft, but still edible.

This plate of spaghetti was actually passable.

"You made this?" Her expression was intriguing.

Chris was secretly pleased, almost unable to suppress a smile,coughed,and raised an eyebrow at her, "Is it delicious?"
